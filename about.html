Our Mission & Vision

UCD Recycling operates plastic recycling and PET sheet production activities on a 22,600 m² industrial facility. Our operations cover PET bottle recycling, high-purity flake production and industrial-scale PET sheet extrusion, supplying manufacturers with consistent, export-ready materials.

Mission

Our mission is to transform post-consumer PET waste into high-quality rPET flakes and PET sheets, enabling manufacturers to reduce environmental impact without compromising performance or supply reliability.

Our mission is to turn waste into value — creating cleaner materials for a cleaner future.

— UCD Management

Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted international supplier of recycled PET materials and PET sheets, setting new standards in quality, scale and circular manufacturing.
